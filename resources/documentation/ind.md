## Departure Procedures
- Departures shall be climbed to 13,000 or lower
- Handoffs shall be initiated around 6000 feet
- DAWNN# departures may be cleared direct DAWNN
- ROCKY# departures must join at or before TTH
- MEARZ# shall be handed directly to ZAU

## Arrival procedures
- During periods of high volume, the following serves as a good guide for assigning runways.
  - JAKKS, KOLTS, SMUKE, RACYR – 5L/23R
  - CLANG, GIIBS, SNKPT – 5R/23L

## Final procedures
Simultaneous independent approaches are authorized. One runway shall be dedicated as the "high side" to ensure vertical seperation until established on the localizer.
